Overdrive

Multi-media brand designer for the Southern California Tricking Demo Team OVRDRIVE. I created the brand name, logo, and promotional posters for live performances, including an NBA halftime show and multiple Asian cultural events in Orange County. This project involved conceptualizing the visual identity, balancing bold colors and dynamic typography to match the energy and athleticism of the team. Every poster and graphic was designed to energize audiences and reflect the team's high-flying style.
